Privacy Policy – My Year Recap

Last updated: March 2026

My Year Recap respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the application handles your information.

Information Collection

My Year Recap does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

The app does not require account creation and does not track users.

Local Data Storage

All notes and information created within My Year Recap are stored locally on your device using your device’s local storage.

This means:

  • Your data stays on your device.
  • The developer of the app does not have access to your notes or personal entries.
  • No information is transmitted to external servers.

Data Export

The app may allow you to export your data to files stored on your device.

These files are created only when the user explicitly chooses to export their data.

The exported files remain fully under the user’s control.

Third-Party Services

My Year Recap does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on the user’s device, the security of the data depends on the security of the device itself.

Users are responsible for protecting access to their device.

Children's Privacy

My Year Recap does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Because the app does not collect any data, it is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Any updates will be reflected with a new “Last updated” date.
